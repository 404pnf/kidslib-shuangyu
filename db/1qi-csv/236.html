性的魅力 Sex Appeal
作者：D. H. 劳伦斯 by D. H. Lawrence
“性”是一个十分丑陋的小字眼，这多么令人遗憾。一个丑陋的小字眼，实际上也让人几乎很难理解。性到底是什么呢？关于性，我们想的越多，知道的就越少。 It is a pity that sex is such an ugly little word.An ugly little word, and really almost incomprehensible.What is sex, after all?The more we think about it, the less we know.
科学告诉我们，性是一种本能，但本能又是什么呢？显然，本能是一种非常古老的习惯，这习惯已变得根深蒂固。但不管多么古老，习惯总得有个开始。然而就性来说，还真没有开始。哪里有生命，哪里就有性。因此，性绝不是养成的“习惯”。此外，人们把性说成是一种欲望，就像饥饿一样。性是一种欲望，但它是对什么的欲望呢？是繁殖欲吗？这很荒唐。他们说，雄孔雀展开所有漂亮的羽毛，是为了吸引雌孔雀，以满足自己的繁殖欲。但雌孔雀为何不展示自己的漂亮羽毛来吸引雄孔雀，以满足自己的繁殖欲呢？与雄孔雀一样，雌孔雀肯定也十分渴望见到孔雀蛋，渴望见到小孔雀。我们难以相信，雌孔雀的性冲动如此微弱，以至于需要用那些华丽的蓝羽毛来唤起自己的性欲。事实并非如此。就我而言，我从未见过哪只雌孔雀注意过自己丈夫那蓝黄色的光鲜羽毛。我认为它从未注意过。我认为雌孔雀并不知道黄褐色、蓝色、褐色或绿色之间的区别。假如我曾见过雌孔雀全神贯注地注视着自己丈夫艳丽的羽毛，或许我会相信：雄孔雀展示羽毛是为了“吸引”雌孔雀。但雌孔雀从来就没看过它。仅当雄孔雀像树林中的风暴一样，在雌孔雀面前抖动它的翮羽时，雌孔雀才看起来有点儿得意。然后，雌孔雀似乎才偶然地注意到雄孔雀的存在。 Science says it is an instinct: but what is an instinct?Apparently an instinct is an old, old habit that has become ingrained.But a habit, however old, has to have a beginning.And there is really no beginning to sex.Where life is, there it is.So sex is no “habit” that has been formed.Again, they talk of sex as an appetite, like hunger.An appetite: but for what?An appetite for propagation?It is rather absurd.They say a peacock puts on all his fine feathers to dazzle the pea-hen into letting him satisfy his appetite for propagation.But why should the pea-hen not put on fine feathers, to dazzle the peacock, and satisfy her desire for propagation?She has surely quite as great a desire for eggs and chickens as he has.We cannot believe that her sex-urge is so weak that she needs all that blue splendour of feathers to rouse her.Not at all.As for me, I never even saw a pea-hen so much as look at her lord’s bronze and blue glory.I don’t believe she ever sees it.I don’t believe for a moment that she knows the difference between bronze, blue, brown or green.If I had ever seen a pea-hen gazing with rapt attention on her lord’s flamboyancy, I might believe that he had put on all those feathers just to “attract” her.But she just never looks at him.Only she seems to get a little perky when he shudders all his quills at her, like a storm in the trees.Then she does seem to notice, just casually, his presence.
这些性理论多么令人惊异。雄孔雀展示华丽的羽毛，是为了吸引目光斜视、连看都不看它一眼的雌孔雀。想象一下，竟有科学家如此天真，认为雌孔雀具有深厚敏锐的能力来欣赏雄孔雀羽毛的颜色和图案。哦！多么善于审美的雌孔雀啊！ These theories of sex are amazing.A peacock puts on his glory for the sake of a wall-eyed pea-hen who never looks at him.Imagine a scientist being so naїve as to credit the pea-hen with a profound, dynamic appreciation of a peacock’s colour and pattern.Oh, highly-aesthetic pea-hen!
此外，雄夜莺唱歌也是为了吸引雌夜莺。非常奇怪，人们会看到，雄夜莺在求偶期和蜜月结束时唱得最卖力，而那时雌夜莺已经不再注意它，而是用心照顾幼雏去了。那么，如果雄夜莺唱歌不是为了吸引雌性，那它肯定是为了分散雌夜莺的注意力，或者是在雌夜莺孵卵时取悦它。 And a nightingale sings to attract his female.Which is mighty curious, seeing he sings his best when courtship and honeymoon are over, and the female is no longer concerned with him at all, but with her young—Well then, if he doesn’t sing to attract her, he must sing to distract her, or amuse her while she’s sitting.
这些理论是多么有趣，多么天真。但所有这些理论背后都隐藏着一种意志。所有性理论背后都隐藏着一种意志，这种意志是难以消除的。很奇怪，这种意志就是想要否认、消除美的神秘感。因为美是一种神秘的事物。你既不能拿它作食物，也不能用它制造法兰绒——而科学则告诉我们，美只是一种手段，用来俘获雌性，引诱其繁殖罢了。多么天真呀。好像雌性需要引诱一样。雌性甚至会在黑暗中繁殖——那么，以美作手段又体现在哪里呢？ How delightful, how naїve theories are.But there is a hidden will behind them all.There is a hidden will behind all theories of sex, implacable.And that, strangely enough, is the will to deny, to wipe out the mystery of beauty.Because beauty is a mystery. You can neither eat it nor make flannel out of it—Well then, says science, it is just a trick to catch the female and induce her to propagate.How naїve.As if the female needed inducing.She will propagate in the dark, even—so where then is the beauty trick.
科学对美有一种神秘的憎恶之情，因为美不符合因果关系链。社会对性也有一种神秘的憎恶之情，因为对社会人精密的赚钱计划，性永远是一种干扰。于是，这两种憎恶交织在一起，性和美就变得只是繁殖欲望了。 Science has a mysterious hatred of beauty, because it doesn’t fit in the cause-and-effect chain.And society has a mysterious hatred of sex, because it perpetually interferes with the nice money-making schemes of social man.So the two hatreds made a combine, and sex and beauty are mere propagation-appetite.
而现在，性和美浑然一体，就像火焰和火一样。假如你憎恶性，你就是憎恶美。假如你喜欢生命活力之美，你就会对性心怀崇敬。当然，你也可能喜爱老化朽亡之美，对性憎恶万分。但是，要喜欢生命活力之美，你必须对性满怀崇敬。 Now sex and beauty are one thing, like flame and fire.If you hate sex you hate beauty.If you love living beauty, you have a reverence for sex.Of course you can love old dead beauty and hate sex.But to love living beauty you must have a reverence for sex.
性与美不可分割，就像生命与意识一样。有一种智慧与性和美相伴，并且源于性和美，那就是直觉。我们文明的大灾难就是对性的病态憎恶——比如，有什么比弗洛伊德的精神分析学对性表现得更加憎恶不已呢？——弗洛伊德精神分析学对美、对“鲜活”之美怀有一种病态的恐惧，这导致我们直觉官能和直觉自我的萎缩。现代男人和女人的严重心理疾病就是因为直觉官能萎缩而呈现的病态。整个世界到处生机盎然，或许我们凭直觉就能认识并享受这一切，仅凭直觉就可以。但我们却无从认识和欣赏，因为我们否认性与美，而它们正是直觉生活和无忧无虑的源泉，这种无忧无虑在自在的动植物身上显得十分可爱。 Sex and beauty are inseparable, like life and consciousness.And the intelligence which goes with sex and beauty, and arises out of sex and beauty, is intuition.The great disaster of our civilisation is the morbid hatred of sex—what, for example, could show a more poisoned hatred of sex than Freudian psycho-analysis?—which carries with it a morbid fear of beauty, “alive” beauty, and which causes the atrophy of our intuitive faculty and our intuitive self.The deep psychic disease of modern men and women is the diseased condition of the atrophied intuitive faculties.There is a whole world of life that we might know and enjoy by intuition, and by intuition alone.This is denied us, because we deny sex and beauty, the source of the intuitive life and of the insouciance which is so lovely in free animals and in plants.
性是根，直觉是叶，美是花。如果一个女人可爱的话，为何是在二十几岁时显得可爱？那是因为，此时，性悄悄爬上她的脸庞，就像玫瑰在枝头绽放。 Sex is the root of which intuition is the foliage and beauty is the flower.Why is a woman lovely, if ever, in her twenties?It is the time when sex rises softly to her face, as a rose to the top of a rose-bush.
这种魅力就是美的魅力，而我们却处处否定它。我们竭力让美显得尽可能浅薄、无用。但首先，性的魅力就是美的魅力。 And the appeal is the appeal of beauty.We deny it wherever we can.We try to make the beauty as shallow and trashy as possible.But first and foremost, sex appeal is the appeal of beauty.
眼下，我们对美所知甚少，几乎不知怎么谈论它。我们试图声称，美是一种固定的模式：直挺的鼻子、大大的眼睛等等。我们觉得，可爱的女人必须像莉莲·吉什那样，英俊的男人必须像鲁道夫·瓦伦蒂诺那样。我们就是这样认为的。然而在现实生活中，我们表现得大为不同。我们可能会说：她的确挺美，但我并不喜欢她——由此可以看出，我们对“美”这个词的使用完全是错误的。我们应该这样说：她具备美的既定特征，但在我看来，她并不美。 Now beauty is a thing about which we are so uneducated, we can hardly speak of it.We try to pretend it is a fixed arrangement: straight nose, large eyes, etc.We think a lovely woman must look like Lilian Gish, a handsome man must look like Rudolf Valentino.So we think.In actual life, we behave quite differently.We say: She’s quite beautiful, but I don’t care for her—Which shows we are using the word beautiful all wrong.We should say: She has the stereotyped attributes of beauty, but she is not beautiful to me.
美是一种体验，如此而已。美不是固定模式，也不是一系列特征的排列。美是我们所感觉到的某种东西，是一种光辉，或是一种对精致事物的共通感知。令我们倍感痛苦的是，我们对美的感受力受到损伤，变得迟钝，使我们错过许多最美好的事物。但就电影而论——查理·卓别林搞怪的脸上所表现出的本质美比瓦伦蒂诺脸上的更多。卓别林的眉目之间有一些真实的美，那是某种纯真的东西闪现出来的光芒。但我们对美的感受力受损严重，异常愚钝，以致于我们看不见这种美，即使看到了也不知道。我们只能看见那种非常显眼的美，比如所谓的鲁道夫·瓦伦蒂诺的英俊，这种美之所以让我们愉悦，仅是因为它满足了关于英俊的一些既定观念。 Beauty is an experience, nothing else.It is not a fixed pattern or an arrangement of features.It is something felt, a glow, or a communicated sense of fineness.What ails us is that our sense of beauty is so bruised and blunted, we miss all the best.But to stick to the film—there is a greater essential beauty in Charlie Chaplin’s odd face, than ever there was in Valentino’s.There is a bit of true beauty in Chaplin’s brows and eyes, a gleam of something pure.But our sense of beauty is so bruised and clumsy, we don’t see it, and don’t know it when we do see it.We can only see the blatantly obvious, like the so-called beauty of Rudolf Valentino, which only pleases because it satisfies some ready-made notion of handsomeness.
但相貌最普通的人也可以看上去很美，也可以真的很美。要让一张丑陋的脸变得可爱，只需让性的火焰慢慢升腾。美感的传递，才是真正的性的魅力。反过来，没有人会像真正的美女那样令人生厌了。也就是说，没人会像真正的美女那样丑陋无比，因为美是一种体验，而非一种具体形式。缺失了性的火焰，她的一举一动都丑陋冷淡，那么她看起来将是多么可憎，而她外在的美貌会使她看上去更丑。 But the plainest person can look beautiful, can be beautiful.It only needs the fire of sex to rise delicately, to change an ugly face to a lovely one.That is really sex appeal: the communicating of a sense of beauty.And in the reverse way, no-one can be quite so repellant as a really pretty woman.That is, since beauty is a question of experience, not of concrete form, no-one can be as acutely ugly as a really pretty woman.When the sex-glow is missing, and she moves in ugly coldness, how hideous she seems, all the worse for her externals of prettiness.
性是什么，我们无从知晓，但它一定是某种火焰。因为它时刻传递着一种温暖、热烈的感觉。当这种热烈成为一种纯粹的光亮，我们就感受到了美感。 What sex is, we don’t know, but it must be some sort of fire.For it always communicates a sense of warmth, of glow.And when this glow becomes a pure shine, then we feel the sense of beauty.
但是温暖的传递、性的热烈是真正的性的魅力。我们所有人的体内都有性的火焰在沉睡，或者在燃烧。如果我们活到90岁，性的火焰依然不会熄灭。否则，也就是说假如性的火焰熄灭了，我们就会变成一具可怖的活尸。不幸的是，世界上这种活尸变得越来越多。没有什么比一个性的火焰已熄灭的人更加丑陋。这样的人是一个令人生厌的泥人，人人避之不及。 But the communicating of the warmth, the glow of sex is true sex appeal.We all have the fire of sex slumbering or burning inside us.If we live to be ninety, it is still there.Or, if it dies, we become one of those ghastly living corpses which unfortunately are becoming more numerous in the world.Nothing is more ugly than a human being in whom the fire of sex has gone out.You get a nasty clayey creature whom everybody wants to avoid.
不过，在我们完好地活着时，性的火焰就会在我们体内闷烧或燃烧着。我们年轻时，它闪烁着，照耀着；我们年老时，它依然发着光热，更为柔和安静，但它没有熄灭。我们对它有一定控制力，但控制力有限。这就是社会憎恶它的原因。性的火焰是美和愤怒之源，尽管它长久不灭，在我们体内燃烧着，我们还是无法理解它。就像真正的火焰，它燃烧时，如果我们不小心碰到了，它就会灼伤我们的手指。因此，仅想着“安全”的社会人，只会憎恶性的火焰。 But while we are fully alive, the fire of sex smoulders or burns in us.In youth it flickers and shines, in age it glows softer and stiller, but there it is.We have some control over it; but only partial control.That is why society hates it.While ever it lives, the fire of sex, which is the source of beauty and anger, burns in us beyond our understanding.Like actual fire, while it lives it will burn our fingers if we touch it carelessly.And so social man, who only wants to be “safe”, hates the fire of sex.
所幸，在只做社会人方面，成功的男性并不多。人类的原罪之火仍在闷烧着。火焰的一个特点是它会蔓延。这里的性之火焰会点燃那里的性之火焰。或许，它只是让闷烧变成柔和的光热。或许，它会让火光明亮闪烁。或许，它会引发火焰，然后火焰连成一片，熊熊燃烧起来。 Luckily not many men succeed in being merely social men.The fire of the old Adam smoulders.And one of the qualities of fire is that it calls to fire.Sex-fire here kindles sex-fire there.It may only rouse the smoulder into a soft glow.It may call up a sharp flicker.Or it may rouse a flame: and then flame leans to flame, and starts a blaze.
不管何时，性的火焰由内而外散发光热时，它将在别处某个地方引起回应。或许，它只是引起一丝温暖和乐观的感觉。这时你会说：我喜欢那个女孩，她确实很不错。或许，它会引燃一团火光，这让世界看起来更亲切，让人感觉生活更美好。这时你会说：她是个迷人的女人，啊，我喜欢她——或者，她可能引燃一团火焰，先使她自己的脸庞焕发光彩，然后再照亮整个宇宙。这时你会说：她是个可爱的女人。在我眼中，她很可爱。我们不必再多说了——很少有女人能引起真正可爱的感觉。女人并非天生就美丽。我们这样说，是为了跳出自己对美的拙劣的、受损的、愚笨的理解。历史上，像迪亚娜·德普瓦克蒂尔丝或者兰特里夫人那样出名的漂亮女人有成千上万个。今天，看起来漂亮无比的女人也成千上万。但是，可爱的女人是多么少见！ Whenever the sex fire glows through, it will kindle an answer somewhere or other.It may only kindle a sense of warmth and optimism.Then you say: I like that girl; she’s a real good sort.It may kindle a glow, that makes the world look kindlier, and life feel better.Then you say: She’s an attractive woman, by Jove, I like her—Or she may rouse a flame that lights up her own face first, before it lights up the universe.Then you say: She’s a lovely woman.She looks lovely to me.Let’s say no more—It takes rather a rare woman to rouse a real sense of loveliness.It is not that a woman is born beautiful.We say that to escape our own poor, bruised, clumsy understanding of beauty.There have been thousands and thousands of women quite as good-looking as Diane de Poictiers or Mrs. Langtry or any of the famous ones.There are today thousands and thousands of superbly good-looking women.But oh, how few lovely women!
这是为什么呢？因为缺乏性魅力。一个漂亮女人，只有当她体内燃起纯洁美好的性之火焰，并且火光在她脸上闪烁，点燃我体内的火焰时，她才变得可爱。这时，在我看来她就变成一位可爱的女人，这时她才是一个有血有肉的可爱女人，而非只是一个可爱女人的照片。这是多么可爱的女人啊！多么可爱！但是，天呐！这样的女人多么稀少啊！这个世界到处都是非常俊俏的女孩和女人，可爱的女人多么少见。 And why?Because of the failure of their sex-appeal.A good-looking woman only becomes lovely when the fire of sex rouses pure and fine in her, and flickers through her face and touches the fire in me.Then she becomes a lovely woman to me, then she is in the living flesh a lovely woman: not a mere photograph of one.And how lovely, a lovely woman!But alas, how rare!How bitterly rare, in a world full of unusually handsome girls and women.
俊俏、漂亮，但并不可爱，也不美。俊俏漂亮的女人是具有良好的相貌和中看的头发的女人。而可爱的女人是一种体验。这是火焰蔓延的问题。用我们贫乏、无力的现代措辞来说，这是性的魅力的问题。性的魅力！——用在迪亚娜·德普瓦克蒂尔丝身上——甚至在欢乐时刻，用在自己妻子身上！为什么性魅力本身是一种侮辱和诽谤？ Handsome, good-looking, but not lovely, not beautiful.Handsome and good-looking women are the women with good features and the right hair.But a lovely woman is an experience.It is a question of communicated fire.It is a question of sex appeal, in our poor, delapidated modern phraseology.Sex appeal!—applied to Diana de Poictiers!—or even, in the lovely hours, to one’s own wife!Why it is a libel and a slander in itself.
然而如今，可爱之火没了，取而代之的是性的魅力。我想，两者是一回事，但却属于不同的层面。商界男士那漂亮、忠实的女秘书依然非常有价值，主要是因为她们的性魅力。但这丝毫不意味着存在什么“不道德关系”。即使在今天，一个略微慷慨的女孩也乐于感觉自己在帮助一位男士，如果这位男士愿意接受她的帮助的话。这位男士应该接受她的帮助，这种渴望表现出来就是她的性魅力。即便散发出很少一点点热量，它也是真正的火焰。它依然发挥着作用，维持着“商业”界的运转。很可能，如果不是在商界男士的办公室引入女秘书，男性商人或许早已彻底垮掉了。女秘书唤起了自己体内神圣的火焰，并将这种火焰传递给了她的老板。由此，他感到额外的精力和乐观精神在体内涌动——他的事业也因此兴旺发达。这或许是性的魅力在当今的最好结果——事业兴旺。 Nowadays, however, instead of the fire of loveliness it is sex appeal.The two are the same thing, I suppose, but on vastly different levels.The business-man’s pretty and devoted secretary is still chiefly valuable because of her sex appeal.Which does not imply “immoral relations” in the slightest.Even today, a girl with a bit of generosity likes to feel she is helping a man, if the man will take her help.And this desire that he shall take her help is her sex appeal.It is the genuine fire, if of a very mediocre heat.Still, it serves to keep the world of “business” alive.Probably, but for the introduction of the lady secretary into the business-man’s office, the business-man would have collapsed entirely by now.She calls up the sacred fire in her, and she communicates it to her boss.He feels an added flow of energy and optimism, and—business flourishes.That is perhaps the best result of sex appeal today—business flourishes.
当然，性的魅力也有另一面——它也可能毁掉被吸引的人。当一个女人开始用她的性魅力为自己牟取优势时，这对某个可怜的家伙来说，往往不是好事。但性魅力的这一面近来被过度使用，所以它的危险性不及以前。巴尔扎克作品中那些毁掉了很多男人的、具有性吸引力的情妇现在已不再吃香。男人们已变得谨慎起来。即使是感情上的勾引，他们也避而远之。实际上，如今男人们倾向于认为，他们一觉察到女性的性魅力，就会感到不妙。 There is, of course, the other side of sex-appeal—it can be the destruction of the one appealed to.When a woman starts using her sex-appeal for her own advantage, it is usually a bad moment for some poor devil.But this side of sex appeal has been overworked lately, so it is not nearly so dangerous as it was.The sex-appealing courtesans who ruined so many men in Balzac no longer find it smooth running.Men have grown canny.They fight shy even of the emotional vamp.In fact, men are inclined to think they smell a rat the moment they feel the touch of feminine sex appeal today.
这多么令人遗憾啊，因为性魅力只不过是一点生命之火的不体面称呼。对男人们来说，当某位女性在他的血脉里点燃一团火苗时，他表现得最出色、最成功。对女人来说，只有她沉浸在爱河中时，她才会把做家务当作真正的快乐——当然，一个女人也许在爱中平静地度过了50年，还几乎不知道爱的存在。如果我们的文明教会了我们如何让性魅力恰当、巧妙地流淌，如何让性的火焰保持纯净不灭，并让其以各种不同的旺盛程度和传播力度闪烁、燃烧、炽燃，那么我们所有人可能都会在爱中生活，这就意味着这种生活从各个方面，在任何事情上，都是在燃烧着的，是充满激情的。然而，我们现今的生活中却布满了死灰！ Which is a pity, for sex appeal is only a dirty name for a bit of life-flame.No man works so well and so successfully, as when some woman has kindled a little fire in his veins.No woman does her housework with real joy unless she is in love—and of course, a woman may go on being quietly in love for fifty years, almost without knowing it.If only our civilisation had taught us how to let sex appeal flow properly and subtly, how to keep the fire of sex clear and alive, flickering or glowing or blazing in all its varying degrees of strength and communication, we might all of us have lived all our lives in love, which means kindled and full of zest, in all kinds of ways and for all kinds of things.Whereas what a lot of dead ash there is to life now!
